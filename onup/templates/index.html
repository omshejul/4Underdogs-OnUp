About Us

Have you ever had a billion dollar idea? You think about it pretty far ahead but get stuck at one point, i.e, FUNDING!

These is where we come to help you. Welcome to OnUp, a site where we connect patrons to thinkers, so you do not have to worry about funding anymore.

Roles

Have an understanding of the various roles and the work they peroform, click on the icon to register for that particular role.

Community Builders

Community builders are organizers for the event. Universities or other entities that are ready to host offline event for the pitch fall under this role. Click on the icon to register for this role.

Patrons

Patrons are donators, who will judge the pitch by the program developers and will vote for the best pitch, who will then recieve the prize pool which is a contribution of the money offered by the donators. Click on the icon to register for this role.

Project Developers

\Need funding for the brilliant idea you though of? Click on the icon to register start pitching to impress the patrons and get funding for your idea.
